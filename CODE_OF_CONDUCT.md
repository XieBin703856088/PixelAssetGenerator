# 贡献者公约 / Contributor Covenant

## 我们的承诺 / Our Pledge

为了营造一个开放、友好的社区环境，我们作为贡献者和维护者承诺：
无论年龄、体型、残疾、种族、性别认同、经验水平、国籍、个人外貌、种族、宗教或性取向，
参与我们的项目和社区的每个人都不会受到骚扰。

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## 我们的标准 / Our Standards

**促进积极环境的行为：**
- 使用友善和包容的语言
- 尊重不同观点和经验
- 优雅地接受建设性批评
- 关注对社区最有利的事情
- 对其他社区成员表示同理心

**Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

**不可接受的行为：**
- 使用性暗示语言或图像，以及不受欢迎的性关注
- 挑衅、侮辱/贬低性评论，以及人身或政治攻击
- 公开或私下的骚扰
- 未经明确许可发布他人的私人信息
- 在专业环境中可能被合理认为不适当的其他行为

**Examples of unacceptable behavior:**
- The use of sexualized language or imagery, and unwelcome sexual attention
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## 执行 / Enforcement

项目维护者有责任明确执行本准则。
如有违规行为，请通过 Issue 或邮件联系维护者。
所有投诉将被及时、公正地审查和调查。

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct.
Instances of abusive behavior may be reported by opening an Issue or contacting the maintainers.
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
